Abstract




Our product, Hatify is a revolutionary hat. The product aims towards helping people who are not blessed with the gift of vision; also it helps those who have given up their vision to the virtual world: recording lectures, speeches or those boring meeting where you would be forced not to sleep. This device would enable you to keep track of everything around you. But how can we forget about those fancy health measuring tools, this device has also been incorporated with a pulse rate sensor and temperature sensing unit to monitor your daily activities on the go. Keeping track of surrounding would never be this simple again. Business meeting? Lectures? How about spying the corrupt? Why carry an extra recorder when a hat could do that all those kinds of stuff for you. Our ideal user is ----YOU----!!!

Working :

Put on the hat, thanks to the autostart feature it will start working and log your vitals in the SD card and warn you about the obstacles on the path so that you can walk carefree.
Press the only button on hat to start the audio recording for taking audio recording or taking evidence ;)
To switch off the hat just remove it, hat will go into energy saver mode. If you want to show your logged vitals just take out the SD card and insert it into your PC, find the raw data in TXT format.
